Prudential – Individual Life Insurance Platform

Product Designer | 2020–2021 (1.5 years)

Conception and design of a new individual life insurance sales platform, integrating partners such as Itaú, XP, and BTG. Worked on quantitative and qualitative research, persona definition, journey mapping, ideation, and prototyping.

Key Achievements:

  • Created 10 personas and complete sales journey mapping
  • Led workshops with 64 participants to prioritize features
  • Prototyping and testing with consultants and wholesalers
  • Achieved usability improvements and reduced average policy issuance cycle to 11 days

Wher – Safe Navigation App for Women

UX/UI Designer | 2018–2019 | Turin, Italy

Experience and interface design for the first app that suggests safe routes for women, based on collaborative assessments of streets and regions.

Key Achievements:

  • Research with 178 women across 6 European cities (Milan, Turin, Catania, London, Barcelona, and Berlin)
  • Created personas and empathy maps to guide design decisions
  • Structured screen flows and user journeys for improved navigation clarity
  • App launched with 4.7-star average rating in app stores

Revista Ferroviária – Responsive Website

UX Researcher, UX/UI Designer, Frontend & Backend Developer | 2018

Complete redesign of Revista Ferroviária website, modernizing the interface while preserving the essence of the previous version.

Key Achievements:

  • Research with 459 users (subscribers, newsletter readers, and visitors)
  • Identified three main personas (CEO, young engineer, and commercial manager)
