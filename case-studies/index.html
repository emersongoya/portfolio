Dell Technologies UX Transformation

Role: Lead UX Designer Year: 2020-2021 Type: Enterprise Design System

Challenge

Dell needed to modernize their enterprise design system to support rapid product development across multiple teams while maintaining brand consistency.

Approach

  • Conducted comprehensive audit of existing design patterns
  • Facilitated workshops with stakeholders across 15+ product teams
  • Developed scalable component library with design tokens
  • Created comprehensive documentation and governance model

Outcomes

  • 40% reduction in design-to-development time
  • Unified design language across 20+ products
  • Increased team productivity and cross-team collaboration

Prudential Enterprise Portal Redesign

Role: Senior UX Designer Year: 2021-2022 Type: B2B Platform

Challenge

Redesign a complex B2B portal to improve usability and streamline workflows for insurance agents managing client portfolios.

Approach

  • User research with 50+ insurance agents across markets
  • Journey mapping and pain point analysis
  • Iterative prototyping and usability testing
  • Collaboration with development teams for implementation

Outcomes

  • 60% reduction in task completion time
  • 35% increase in user satisfaction scores
  • Decreased support tickets by 45%

OnAir SaaS Platform Optimization

Role: Product Designer Year: 2019-2020 Type: Startup Product

Challenge

Optimize the user onboarding experience for a communication platform targeting small businesses, addressing high drop-off rates.

Approach

  • Analytics analysis to identify friction points
  • A/B testing of onboarding flows
  • User interviews with churned and active users
  • Progressive disclosure strategy for complex features

Outcomes

  • 75% improvement in onboarding completion rate
  • 50% reduction in time-to-value
  • 30% increase in user activation within first week
