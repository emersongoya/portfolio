About

Two Decades of Design Excellence

With over 20 years of experience in product design, I've led transformative projects across enterprise platforms, SaaS products, and global design systems. My expertise spans from strategic design thinking to hands-on interface craftsmanship.

I specialize in bridging the gap between business objectives and user needs, creating design solutions that are not only beautiful but drive measurable business impact. My work encompasses UX research, design systems architecture, and team leadership.

Throughout my career, I've collaborated with Fortune 500 companies, tech startups, and everything in between, always maintaining a focus on creating intuitive, accessible, and delightful user experiences.
